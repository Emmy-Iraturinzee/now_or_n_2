About us

Innovating Today to empower Smarter and Sustainable Tomorrow.

We are a forward-thinking IT and management consulting firm helping organizations unlock digital transformation and business growth. Our expertise spans Digital Governance, E-Agriculture, Digital Trade, and Digital Health & eHealth Solutions, empowering public and private sectors to harness technology for smarter service delivery. Through a holistic approach—combining strategy, operations, financial advisory and Fintech Innovation, HR consulting, change management, marketing & sales consulting, —we deliver results that drive efficiency, resilience, and sustainable impact.

Partner with us to shape your digital future with smart solutions that bridge technology and management excellence.

Our history

At NOW OR NEVER LTD, we are more than just consultants, we are architects of innovative, future-ready solutions. Established in 2021, we have been at the forefront of empowering businesses with tailored, efficient, and technology-driven solutions. Based in Kigali, Rwanda, we are a registered and licensed consultancy committed to fostering sustainable growth and success for our clients.
