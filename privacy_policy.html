Privacy Policy

We collect data when you create an account, make a purchase, or interact with our services. Data is collected through forms, cookies, and tracking pixels on our website.

We collect personal information including name, email address, phone number, payment details, browsing history, IP address, and device information for service improvement and security purposes.

Data is collected to provide services, improve user experience, process transactions, send communications, analyze usage patterns, and comply with legal obligations.

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data. You can opt-out of marketing communications and request data portability at any time through your account settings.

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children and will delete any such information immediately upon discovery.

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal requirements. Most data is deleted within 2 years of account inactivity.

Data is stored on encrypted servers with advanced security measures. We use firewalls, SSL protocols, and regular security audits to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Your data may be shared with payment processors, analytics providers, and service vendors. All third parties are bound by data protection agreements and comply with privacy laws.

We use cookies for authentication, preferences, and analytics. You can control cookie settings in your browser. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for site functionality.

We do not sell your personal information. Sharing occurs only with your consent, for service delivery, or when legally required. You can control sharing preferences in your privacy settings.

For privacy inquiries, contact us at privacy@nowornever.com or mail to: Privacy Team, KN 3RD, 2nd Floor Kigali, Rwanda. Response time is typically 5-7 business days.
