Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries about our assessment framework.

Who is this service for?

Our Digital Maturity Assessment is designed for government institutions, public agencies, NGOs, and private sector organizations across Africa that are planning or currently executing a digital transformation journey and need an objective, evidence-based baseline to guide their decisions.

How long does an assessment take?

A standard end-to-end engagement runs 6–8 weeks from kick-off to final roadmap delivery. The timeline varies based on organization size, number of departments included, and data availability.

Who should be involved from our organization?

We typically engage a core team of 4–6 people covering IT, operations, HR, and a senior executive sponsor. Broader staff participation happens through surveys and focus groups during the assessment phase.

Is the framework adapted for the African context?

Yes. While our core model follows international best practice, the scoring benchmarks, sector comparisons, and roadmap recommendations are calibrated specifically for the East and Central African operating environment — accounting for infrastructure realities, regulatory context, and local capacity.

Can we re-assess after implementing the roadmap?

Absolutely. Many clients run a re-assessment every 12–18 months to track progress, update priorities, and refresh the roadmap in line with new organizational goals and market conditions.

Does NoN support implementation after the assessment

Yes. We offer optional execution support packages covering project management, vendor selection, change management, and technology implementation — so the roadmap doesn't just sit on a shelf..
